About Us

At Zeytuun, we bring the rich and diverse flavours of Somali cuisine to the heart of London. Located at 106A Kilburn High Road, London NW6 4HY, we pride ourselves on offering an authentic and welcoming dining experience.

Zeytuun Restaurant was established with a vision to bring the rich and diverse flavours of Somali cuisine to the heart of London. Inspired by a deep passion for food and a desire to share Somalia’s culinary heritage, Zeytuun opened its doors as a family-run business, combining traditional recipes passed down through generations with a modern dining experience. Located on Kilburn High Road, we take pride in offering a warm and welcoming atmosphere, where every dish is crafted with the finest ingredients and authentic spices. From our signature Bariis and Laxoox to the beloved Sambusa, Zeytuun is more than a restaurant—it’s a celebration of culture, community, and the timeless joy of good food.
